**Meaning**:
was just about to (do something)

**Grammar**:
<u>AVst</u>(으)려던 참이었다

**Usage**:
Expresses that you were on the verge of doing something (but haven't yet). Often implies the action was interrupted or hasn't happened yet. Used when explaining timing or why something didn't happen.

**Notes:**
- Subject must be capable of intention

**Related Patterns:**
[[V+(으)려]], [[V+던]], [[V+(었,았,였)어(요)]]
