**Yonsei Signature:**
-어야지요/아야지요/여야지요

**Meaning**:
should, ought to (casual agreement/acknowledgment)

**Grammar**:
<u>Vst</u>(어,아,여)야지요.
<u>Vst</u>(었,았,였)어야지요.
<u>N</u>(이)어야지요.

**Usage**:
More casual and conversational than -(어,아,여)야 하다/되다. Often expresses agreement with an obvious truth or acknowledgment. More gentle than -(어,아,여)야 돼 (must/have to).
