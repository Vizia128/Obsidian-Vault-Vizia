**Meaning**:
looks like, appears to be

**Grammar**:
<u>DVst</u>(어,아,여) 보이다.
<u>DVst</u>(었,았,였)어 보이다.

**Usage**:
Expresses visual appearance or impression based on what the speaker sees. The observer is making a judgment based on visual observation.

**Notes:**
- Subject is usually third person

**Related Patterns:**
[[V+(어,아,여)]], [[V+(었,았,였)어(요)]]
