**Meaning**:
Gentle refutation

**Grammar**:
<u>Vst</u>기는(요).

**Usage**:
Expression is used to politely refute or disagree with the other person's statement. Used in response to a compliment to show modesty. Can also be used lightly criticize or scold someone.
