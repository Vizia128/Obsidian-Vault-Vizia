**Yonsei Signature:**
-기만 하다

**Meaning**:
only do, just do, do nothing but

**Grammar**:
<u>Vst</u>기만 하다.
<u>Vst</u>(었,았,였)기만 하다.

**Usage**:
Emphasizes exclusivity - this action/state and nothing else. Often has a negative connotation when criticizing someone's behavior. Stronger emphasis than just -만 (only).

**Related Patterns:**
