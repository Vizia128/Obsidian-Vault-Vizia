**Meaning**:
do and leave (in that state), do in advance (for later use)

**Grammar**:
<u>AVst</u>(어,아,여) 놓다.

**Usage**:
Emphasizes preparation, completion, or the resulting state being maintained. Implies the result is useful or intentional for future reference.

**Related Patterns:**
