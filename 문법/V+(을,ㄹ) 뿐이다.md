**Yonsei Signature:**
-을/ㄹ 뿐이다

**Meaning**:
Only, just, nothing but

**Grammar**:
<u>Vst</u>(을,ㄹ) 뿐이다
<u>Vst</u>(었,았,였)을 뿐이다
<u>N</u>일 뿐이다

**Usage**:
Emphasizes limitation or exclusivity - "this and nothing more." Can express either resignation ("I can only...") or minimization ("I just..."). More formal/literary than -만.
