**Yonsei Signature:**
-ㄹ지/을지 -ㄹ지/을지

**Meaning**:
whether to... or to... (deliberation between options)

**Grammar**:
<u>AVst</u>(을,ㄹ)지 <u>AVst</u>(을,ㄹ)지 \_\_\_\_

**Usage**:
Expresses indecision or uncertainty between two or more mutually exclusive options. Often followed by 모르다 (don't know) or 고민하다 (worry/deliberate).
